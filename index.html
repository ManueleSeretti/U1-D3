Focaccia with caramelized Onion, Cranberries, and Brie

wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped whit sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

FEATURED IN:
fresh cranberry that go beyond sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1 1/4 extra virgin olive oil, plus more the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablepoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut intro cubes (scarpe rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen eranberries

Method

  1. Make the foccia dough In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredient
